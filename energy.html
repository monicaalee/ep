So why should you reduce your water usage?


FACTS:

Every minute a child dies of a water-related disease.

Woman and children spend 200 million hours a day collecting water.

1 out of 9 people lack access to safe water.

There are more mobile phones than toilets in the world.

For every $1 spent on water and sanitation, there's a $4 economic return.



Here are some other ways to help out:


TAKE THE 4 LITERS CHALLENGE

For 24 hours, use just four liters of water for everything you do.
